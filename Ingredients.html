Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Flour 2 1/4 cup
Baking soda 1 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Butter, softened 1 cup(2 sticks)
Granulated sugar 3/4 cup
Packed Brown Sugar 3/4 cup
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Eggs 2 (large)
Chocolate Chips 2 cups (12 ounces package)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 F
  2. Combine all dry ingredients in a small bowl
  3. Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla in large mixer bowl until creamy
  4. Add eggs to butter mixture one at a time, beating well after each addition
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ingredients. Stir in chocolate chips
  6. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheet
  7. Bake for 9-11 minutes, until bottoms begin to turn golden brown
  8. Cool on sheets for 2 mintues, then transfer to wire rack
